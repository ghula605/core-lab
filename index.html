ABOUT

Ali Waqar is a graphic designer and artist based in New York and Muscat. He is interested in the interaction bet-ween typography and image as well as the development of solutions for multilingual design projects, especially in Arabic and English. Waqar studies at Parsons School of Design where he is on track to graduate with a BFA in Communication Design.
