ABOUT/ARABIC

Ali Waqar is a graphic designer and artist based in New York and Muscat. He is interested in the inter-action between typography and image as well as the development of solutions for multi-lingual design projects, especially in Arabic and English. Waqar studies at Parsons School of Design where he is on track to graduate with a BFA in Communication Design.
